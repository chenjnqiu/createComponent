# Badge 徽标

## 参数

<Props of={Badge} />

## 基本用法
<Playground>
     <div>
        <Badge count={5}>
            <Icon type="bell-o" className='demo-cont' />
        </Badge>   
    </div>
</Playground>

## 独立徽标，展示在右侧
<Playground>
     <div>
        <span>店铺消息</span>
        <Badge count={100} />
    </div>
</Playground>

## 设置最大信息数
<Playground>
     <div className='demo-Badge-max'>
        <Badge count={99}>
            <Icon type="bell-o" className='demo-cont' />
        </Badge>
        <Badge count={120}>
            <Icon type="bell-o" className='demo-cont' />
        </Badge>
        <Badge count={120}>
            <Icon type="bell-o" className='demo-cont' />
        </Badge>
    </div>
</Playground>

## 设置是否显示消息数0
<Playground>
     <div className='demo-Badge-max'>
        <Badge count={0} showZero={true}>
            <Icon type="bell-o" className='demo-cont' />
        </Badge>
        <Badge count={0}>
            <Icon type="bell-o" className='demo-cont' />
        </Badge>
    </div>
</Playground>
